Health & Safety of Your Pet

It is recommended that male dogs should not be bread until they are 18 months of age & the female canines should not mate until they are on their second or third heat cycle. Normally, natural mating is conducted at the male dog’s residence. This is because the male outside the normal environment may feel insecure. Pets should be given a private enclosed area with good footing where they can mate without distraction. Both the pet parents should be present at the site. Dogs may take hours or days to feel comfortable with each other based upon their disposition, experience & timing of the breeding. Pets should be supervised at all times so as to ensure their safety. Pet parents can softly talk to pets in order to encourage them and help them feel secure & comfortable. Never holler at them out of frustration.
